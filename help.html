Help & Support

Welcome to Chase Bank Help & Support. Here you’ll find guidance and information to help you manage your account, complete transactions, and stay secure while banking online.

Account Access & Sign-In

If you’re having trouble signing in, ensure your login details are entered correctly and that your device has a stable internet connection. For security reasons, multiple unsuccessful sign-in attempts may temporarily limit access.

Transfers, Payments & Requests

Before completing any transaction, review all details carefully. You will be asked to confirm recipient information, amounts, and notes prior to authorization using your transaction PIN.

Transaction Status

Once a transaction is completed, a confirmation receipt will be generated. You can review transaction details within your account activity section.

Security & Fraud Protection

Chase Bank monitors account activity for unusual or unauthorized behavior. If you notice anything unfamiliar, we recommend taking immediate action to protect your account.

Privacy & Data Protection

Your personal and financial information is handled in accordance with strict privacy and data protection standards. For more details, please review our Privacy Policy.

Additional Assistance

For further guidance, please review the relevant sections of this website, including our Security, Privacy, and Terms & Conditions pages.
